About DKG Labs

Innovation Meets Excellence

Founded by IIT alumni and led by industry veterans from global enterprises, DKG Labs builds end-to end solutions at the intersection of Generative AI and the Internet of Things (IoT). We design intelligent products and platforms that connect devices, stream real-time data, and transform it into actionable insights using advanced AI spanning prompt engineering, model fine-tuning, multimodal analytics, and responsible deployment. Our expertise covers sensor integration, edge computing, secure data pipelines, MLOps, and enterprise-grade architectures that meet compliance and scalability requirements. Beyond delivery, we partner with clients to accelerate adoption through rapid prototypes, production rollouts, and capability building including our Career Launch Program with live industry projects, so organizations can innovate faster, operate smarter, and create measurable business impact.
