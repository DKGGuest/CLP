Program Structure

4-Month Hybrid Learning Journey

Our Career Launch Program blends flexible online learning with hands-on, instructor-led workshops to deliver end-to-end training in today’s most in-demand technologies. Participants build a strong foundation in software engineering while gaining practical expertise in Internet of Things (IoT) systems—sensor integration, device connectivity, and real-time data pipelines and Generative AI, including prompt engineering, model fine-tuning, and responsible deployment. Guided capstone projects, industry case studies, and mentorship translate theory into job-ready skills, and our career support—portfolio building, mock interviews, and placement assistance helps you confidently step into roles across product, data, and engineering teams.

Phase 1: Foundation

45 Days (Online)

Core Modules

  • Programming Fundamentals (Python/Java)
  • IoT Basics & Architecture
  • Cloud Computing Essentials
  • IT Consulting Fundamentals
  • Data Structures & Algorithms

Learning Format

  • Weekly live sessions with mentors
  • Access to DKG Labs' learning platform
  • Online case studies and simulations
  • Self-paced learning modules

Phase 2: Advanced

45 Days (Hybrid)

Live Industry Projects

  • Real-world problem solving
  • Team-based project development
  • Client requirement analysis
  • Solution design and implementation

Offline Workshops

  • Two immersive 3-day intensive training modules
  • Hands-on IoT hardware interaction
  • Hackathons and competitions
  • Client simulation exercises

Mentorship

  • Bi-weekly one-on-one sessions
  • Project leader guidance
  • Career counseling
  • Industry expert insights
